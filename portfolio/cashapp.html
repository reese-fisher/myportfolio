CashApp

Summary

The project goal was to design a feature for customers to easily divide payments to other CashApp members so that all parties are aware of what they're paying for.

The Problem

Cash App users from various backgrounds may struggle to split payments, forget to pay people, or forget to ask for payments due. Our solution would offer a way for users to easily split payments among other users, schedule recurring payments through a trustworthy platform, and track previous payments.

Key Drivers & Deliverables
