Whiskey Dynamite

Summary

Introducing Quixotic's latest production, Whiskey Dynamite. This website has been created to promote the show and guide interested individuals towards purchasing tickets.​

​

Quixotic is an innovative performance art collective that fuses imagination with technology, dance, projection mapping, and live music to create fully-immersive, multi-sensory experiences. The ensemble was given Silicon Valley’s unofficial stamp of genius when they were invited to perform at the global TED Conference in 2012. A unique blend of performance art, technology, and emotion. Quixotic fuses all these elements into what The New York Times branded an “innovative circus performance,” and Men’s Journal declared “one of the best contemporary [cirque] acts.” They have performed for audiences worldwide and have helped launch brand products for companies such as BVLGARI, Faena Hotels, Garmin, HP, Smithsonian, and Toyota.

The Problem

With Whiskey Dynamite in production, Quixotic needed a quickly built site to start promoting and selling tickets for their next show. With the small timeline, Squarespace was the chosen tool I used. The site is all on one page with call-to-action buttons taking you offsite to the ticket sales office.

Key Drivers & Deliverables

They received a fully operational website that catered to their immediate requirements.
